About Us

Our History

Founded in [Year], Happy Tails began as a small local initiative aimed at rescuing and rehoming pets in need. Our mission was to provide a safe haven for animals and to find them loving homes where they could thrive. Over the years, we have grown into a recognized organization dedicated to animal welfare, with a network of volunteers and supporters who share our passion.

Our Mission

At Happy Tails, our mission is to improve the lives of abandoned and neglected pets through compassionate care, effective rehoming, and community outreach. We believe that every pet deserves a chance at a better life and work tirelessly to ensure that each animal in our care finds a loving forever home.
